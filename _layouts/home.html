Who am I?

I am from Slovenia and I enjoy (mostly) math and coding. I'm currently pursuing a bachelors degree in Mathematics on faculty of Mathematics and Physics in Ljubljana.
My main research interest lies in the discrete well-posedness of partial differential equations (PDEs) and stability of time dependent problems. In particular I do research on meshless methods (RBF-FD) and finite element method (FEM) for solving PDEs.
I also like climbing and road cycling in the summer and ski in the winter.
